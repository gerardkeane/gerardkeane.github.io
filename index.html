About

The utopian scholastic aesthetic emerges from a rich tapestry of influences: the meticulous craftsmanship of medieval scriptoriums, the revolutionary spirit of early printing presses, and the democratizing vision of digital encyclopedias. It represents a dream of universal access to knowledge, where classical wisdom meets contemporary innovation.

As a creator in this tradition, I seek to honor both the timeless and the cutting-edge. My work draws inspiration from illuminated manuscripts, academic woodcuts, library card catalogs, and early educational software. Each project aims to capture that magical intersection between ancient wisdom and digital possibility.

This portfolio represents my contribution to this ongoing dialogue between past and future, creating spaces where learning becomes an act of exploration and discovery. Through careful design and thoughtful implementation, I strive to craft experiences that spark curiosity and foster understanding, just as the great libraries and universities have done throughout history.
